WHERE HAS ARRIVED
Tri An Lake is an artificial lake located on Dong Nai River, in the districts of Vinh Cuu, Dinh Quan, Thong Nhat and Trang Bom, Dong Nai province, Vietnam. The lake is a place to store water to supply the Tri An Hydropower Plant. Ho Tri An was started in 1984 and completed in early 1987.
Ke Ga Cape, also known as Khe Ga Cape, is a cape in Tan Thanh commune, Ham Thuan Nam district, Binh Thuan province, Vietnam. This cape juts out in the East Sea, about 40 km southwest of Phan Thiet city, coordinates 10⁰41'42 "north latitude, 107⁰59'8" east longitude.
Lam Dong is one of the five provinces in the Central Highlands, Vietnam, and is also the 7th largest province in the country, bordering the southern key economic region.
